Pedestals are so often used to give a privileged few the opportunity to share their perspective. I hate pedestals for this reason…
It’s a bit corny but… by building our own pedestal, we dictate what we want to put out into the world. And that’s exactly what I’d like you to do. That is if you want to of course.

1/13 - i
This project was originally titled “I Am for a Pedestal” as a spin-off on Claes Oldenburg's writing “I Am for an Art” which states that anything in the real world can be a starting point for art. With this in mind, I was determined to transform the pedestal into more than a mere place holder; I wanted it to inspire art.
